  • - 264 documents from the collections of the Batthyaneum Branch, comprising manuscripts and incunabula;
  • - 149 incunabula;
  • - 617 old Romanian periodicals;
  • - 694 resources of old and bibliophile Romanian book;
  • - 2.331 Romanian and European photographs, Romanian illustrated postcards, illustrated postcards from Bassarabia and Bucovina, photo albums etc .;
  • - 543 manuscripts, including Arabic and Greek manuscripts, as well as documents from the collections of the Batthyaneum Library;
  • - 1.077 resources of old foreign book;
  • - 72 old maps;
  • - 772 sheet music;
  • - 94 exlibris, 107 Japanese stamps, 32 Romanian drawings, 21 engravings, 5 art albums, 9 reference materials;
  • - 636 civil documents: correspondence, time media articles, but also military documents: reports, crochets with the situations of the military units on the battlefield, at different chronological moments; brochures, informative notes, confidential notes, etc., presented in Romania's World War I collection;
  • - 829 resources presented in Virtual Exhibitions about: Al. I. Cuza, First Romanian Astronomical Expedition, Total Eclipse of the Sun from 1893, Romanian Army Campaigns, Dragomir Hurmuzescu, Romanian Women Personalities, etc.
